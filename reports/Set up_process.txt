By operating between your shopping cart and gateway, No Fraud makes integration remarkably easy and virtually compatible with every open source shopping cart platform (as well as many non-open source platforms). Users only need to do two things to go live:

1) Provide No Fraud with your Gateway API ID - This lets No Fraud identify which transactions belong to you so that they are processed accordingly.
2) Change the gateway URL in your platform configuration to route transaction information through No Fraud - Once done, all transactions will be directed to No Fraud for inspection before going to your Gateway.
